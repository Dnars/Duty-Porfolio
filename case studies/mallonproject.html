We had to wait until the movie playing was over for people to leave the cinema because the place was totally filled up

Mendy

Mollan is a movie ticketing platform that offers movie lovers the opportunity to make seat reservations ahead of time.

Target Audience – Movie lovers between age 14 - 65.

Duration – 2 weeks.

My Role – User experience designer, User researcher, user interface designer, visual designer.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

After a long stressfull week at work, Mendy loves to chill out over the weekend with her two friends. During the week, Mendy is seen scanning through websites for newly released movies she could watch, She is a fan of spoilers. But...there is a problem. Mendy & friends likes sticking together even at cinemas, but finding a sitting position that can accommodate all three friends is like mission impossible.

Let’s help Mendy - What if she could choose a sitting position ahead of time and not be worried about being separated from her friends at cinema?

Before that, lets find out what other people are experiencing!

USER RESEARCH

To learn about the problem, I carried out a mix reserach method (quantitative and qualitative research) via survey. The essence was to find out how people deal with poor sitting position and what motivates them to make sat reservations

SURVEY FEEDBACK

EMPATHY MAPPING

The feedbacks I got indicated some people having similar challenge with Mendy. The research even highlighted some key issues not previously thought about. Lets sort the feedbacks using an aggregate empathy map.

Meet Mendy

USER STORIES

What stories do people like Mendy Share?

As a movie lover, I want to be able to find ideal sitting position so that I won’t have to sit in an uncomfortable position when I visit the cinema.

As a student who just want to take a break, I want to find my reserved spot empty so I can sit at the reserved spot when am at the cinema.

As a business administrator, I want to have evidence of my reserved spot should in case I find it occuppied.

As a timid individual, I want the cinema authorities to help inform people to sit according to their reserved position, so there won’t be any conflict of interest.

CUSTOMER JOURNEY MAP

Mapping out Mendy’s journey, from how she goes about finding movies to watch, to visiting the cinema with friends helps me find room for further improvements. How do I help make her journey better?

Mendy, 26

Goal - Find ideal sitting position

Possible Solution

  • A system that shows movie storyline and trailer.
  • A system that allows users buy movie ticket online.
  • A system that can issue tickets and track seats already paid for.
  • Tickets that shows evidence of sitting position.
  • A system that allows users pick convenient sitting position ahead of time.
  • Authorities should ensure users stick to allocated sitting position.

VALUE PROPOSITION

What will Mollan do? - Mollan will allows users buy movie ticket and pick ideal sitting position ahead of time.

What needs will Mollan address?

  • Allow users preview, watch trailers and read movie description within the system.
  • Allow users pick ideal sitting position ahead of time.
  • Give users evidence of sitting position should they find seat occupied.
  • Give users the opportunity to know the available seat(s) and sitting position left in the cinema.

Mollan core value proposition

Comfort

  • Watch movie hightlight
  • Track reserved spots

Convenience

  • Have spot evidence
  • Pick sitting position ahead of time

DESIGN

Low Fidelity Design

Style Guide

Product Screen

Home page - Users get to search trending movies or movies showing in cinema currently within the platform.

Movie Highlights - Users can read more about the movie, watch trailers, cast and location where the movie is been shown in the cinema.

Seat(s) Selections - Users can pick one or more sitting position(s) of their choice ahead of movie time.

Ticket Receipt - Ticket issued contains evidence of seat number which serves as proof of sitting position. Rceipts are auto downloaded to user’s device which is shown to the cinema authorities at point of entry.
